# Results

The system reached homologation and was tested by leadership as an internal workflow for proposal drafting and technical scoping.

Current measurable outcomes are qualitative rather than production-scale metrics:

- Reduced blank-page work for early proposal drafts.
- Centralized references from past projects and architecture patterns.
- Separated LLM reasoning from deterministic pricing logic.
- Created a reusable internal knowledge base for sales engineering decisions.
- Preserved human review before any client-facing proposal.

Recommended next measurements:

- Time from brief to first proposal draft.
- Percentage of proposals requiring senior engineer intervention before first draft.
- Reuse rate of reference architectures.
- Accuracy of estimated scope against final delivered effort.
